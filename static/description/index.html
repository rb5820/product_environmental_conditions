Product Environmental Conditions

Track detailed environmental specifications for your products

The Product Environmental Conditions module enhances your product management capabilities by allowing you to define and track detailed environmental specifications such as temperature ranges, humidity tolerances, altitude limitations, and protection ratings. This ensures that your products meet the necessary standards for various operating conditions. With this module, you can easily document and search products based on their environmental requirements, ensuring compliance with industry standards and improving product reliability. The module also supports IP (Ingress Protection) and IK (Impact) ratings, allowing you to specify the level of protection against dust, water, and mechanical impacts. This comprehensive tracking of environmental conditions helps you ensure that your products are suitable for their intended use, enhancing customer satisfaction and reducing warranty claims.
